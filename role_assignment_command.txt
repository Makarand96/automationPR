//What this command does
Finds the Service Principal github-playwright.
Gets its appId (Client ID).
Gets the Storage Account resource ID.
Assigns the Storage Blob Data Contributor role on that storage account.

After successful execution, your GitHub service principal will be able to upload/download blobs from the storage account, which is commonly needed for Playwright reports, traces, or artifacts.
